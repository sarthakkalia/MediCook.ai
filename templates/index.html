Frequently Asked Questions

How does MediCook.AI work?
MediCook.AI uses artificial intelligence to understand your health conditions, dietary preferences, or available ingredients. Simply enter your requirements, and our AI will generate personalized recipe suggestions to support your health goals.
Is MediCook.AI free to use?
Yes, MediCook.AI is free to use! You can access basic suggestions and nutritional information. We may introduce premium features in the future, but our core functionality will remain free.
Can MediCook.AI help with specific medical conditions?
Yes, our AI is trained to consider various medical conditions and dietary restrictions when generating recipes. However, always consult with your healthcare provider about your specific dietary needs.
